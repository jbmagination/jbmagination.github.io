How to play Mario Kart Wii online

Requirements

You will need:

Either a Wii or Wii U

An SD card

A Mario Kart Wii game disc

Chapter 1: Setting up your SD card

1. Go to please.hackmii.com. It looks phishy, but I can assure you it is perfectly safe to use.

2. Turn on your Wii, and go to "Wii Options".

3. Go to Wii Settings.

4. Go to Internet.

5. Go to Console Information.

6. On the webpage, put in the MAC Address shown on your Wii console.

7. Cut whichever wire you want (it's just a little joke).

8. Make sure your SD card is empty. If it isn't, you may want to put the contents in a folder somewhere (such as your Desktop).

9a. If you are using Windows, download the tool fat32format.

9b. If you are using macOS, open Disk Utility from the Launchpad.
